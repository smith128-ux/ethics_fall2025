Loot boxes come in many different varieties and appearances, but the core of their mechanic remains the same, gambling real money away for in-game items. This close relation to gambling is problematic because of the ways it can harm people's daily lives in ways that traditional gambling might. It is important to recognize the harm that loot boxes can cause to vulnerable players and to take steps towards reforming and addressing these issues.
