layout: default
---

People with a history of gambling problems are especially vulnerable to loot boxes because of the random slot-machine-esque mechanics and reinforcing the cycle of spending money and risk-taking for a desired outcome. Those that are already in financial distress from other gambling habits can be brought to bankruptcy from excessive purchases. The lack of regulation for loot boxes and the accessibility of loot boxes make it difficult to regulate and protect vulnerable people.
