The traditional model of releasing games for a set price has largely fallen out of style amongst large gaming conglomerates. Over the last decade, companies have increasingly realized the importance of incorporating loot boxes into free-to-play games to increase profit compared to a standard set price release. It's usually loot boxes that are responsible for funding and keeping these free-to-play games updated and running. Though, superfluous and unfair loot boxes can often lead to backlash from the public and critics alike.
