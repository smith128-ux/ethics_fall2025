In recent years, policymakers have become increasingly pressured by the public to take action on regulating loot boxes. Because of the international reach of video games, regulation can seem nearly impossible. Policymakers might need to decide whether or not loot boxes should be illegal, or officially declared as gambling. Some countries have already classified loot boxes as a form of gambling.
